LASAGNA

Description

A classic take on lasagne, but with some tweaks that enhance the rich flavour.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Minced pork
  • Minced beef
  • Garlic
  • butter

Steps

  1. Make dough
  2. Brown minced meat
  3. Sautee carrots, onion and celery
  4. Add garlic, tomatoes and white wine
  5. Let it simmer for 3 to 5 hours
  6. add pasta
  7. Put it in the oven for 1 hour
